About Us


Guava Realty Solutions Pvt Ltd was incorporated on 9th February 2019 as a start up venture capital.

We are having an expert team who provides consultancy for Realties | Loans | Insurances | Interiors.

Our organizational values are Trust | Transparency | Integrity | Excellence.


As a realty broker we are licensed by MahaRERA (Maharashtra real estate regulatory authority, Regulation and Development Act 2016).


Our company is Authorized Channel Partner for India’s leading realty companies and holds Loan Connector license for India’s leading PSU & Private banks.

We are also an Industry Partner/Work Lab Partner for MKCL (Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited).



We are currently based out of Navi Mumbai and growing with a robust network through our sales & distributions team across Mumbai | Thane | Raigad locations.


Over the past 12 months, we have been able to achieve the milestone by providing our clients a quality services and we pride ourselves for our best practices in the industry.
